Is your Birthdate a Palindrome?

A palindrome is a word, number, phrase, or other sequence of characters which reads the same backward as forward

How it Works

This web app checks your birthdate in 6 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yy, mm-dd-yyyy , d-mm-yyyy and yy-mm-dd

e.g. if your birthdate is 05 feb 2000, then app will check for 20000205, 05022000, 020500, 02052000, 5022000 and 000205
